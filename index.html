The Story Behind
Every Piece

"Every wall frame, every painting, every shelf in our home — she made it all with her own hands, with a beautiful touch." — Smitarani's mother

Smitarani Mishra's love for art and craft began in childhood — a spark so natural that everything she touched became beautiful. Long before it was a business, it was simply who she was.

She began by making jewellery for herself, quietly and joyfully. When her friends noticed — and began asking her to make pieces for them — the love spread. Word after word, piece after piece, what began as a personal hobby turned into something that touched the lives of hundreds of women across India.

Today, Smitarani's creations are not just jewellery — they are wearable art, each one carrying the warmth of the hands that made them. Her work has been recognised with the prestigious Ananya Award, a testament to her craft and her heart.
